Camping

Camping is an outdoor activity that involves staying the night/more than one night in a protective shelter out in nature. You just need to enjoy yourself and the nature that urrounds you. Receational camping started in the late 19th century and bacame one of the most common outdoor activity all around the world. Camping is all about being in away from the convenience of urban life thus it is important to plan thoroughly what to bring to your camping trip.

What you need for your camping trip

  • A camping Bag
  • A sleeping Bag
  • A flashlight
  • A tent
  • A camping chair
  • A camping stove
  • A trash bag, for the respect of the nature
  • A first aid kit

The benefits of camping

  1. Camping helps with stress reduction
  2. Fresh air has numerous benefits
  3. Camping improves relationships
  4. Camping gives you the opportunity to enjoy great food
  5. Have the chance to connect with nature
