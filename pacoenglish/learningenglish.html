The Importance of learning English

English is the most commonly spoken language in the world.
It is the official language in a total of 67 different countries;
one out of five people can speak or at least understand it.
English increases your chances of getting a good job in a multinational company within your home country or of finding work abroad; learning it can help you meet new people. English is a particularly important language online with
more than half the content on the internet Written in English. It is one of the most important languages for business. With English you can study all over the world. You have access to multiple cultures. This is why you need to learn it today.
Being able to speak it is not just about being about to communicate with native English speakers, it is the most common second language in the world. If you want to speak to someone from another country the chances are that you will both speak English in order to communicate.
